6. Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on cloud infrastructure with robust security measures, including:

Your data may be stored or processed on secure servers located in other countries. When transferring data across borders, we take steps to ensure it is handled in a way that respects local regulations and protects your privacy.

We may use data â€” including personal and farming-related inputs â€” to support activities such as:

  • We do not sell your personal or user-submitted data to third parties.
  • We may offer data-driven insights or aggregated outputs to partners, clients, or stakeholders in a way that does not identify individuals.
  • Any use of data for external purposes is done responsibly and in accordance with industry standards.
