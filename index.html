AgriG8 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

1. Introduction

AgriG8 is committed to protecting your personal and farming data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share data in Cambodia, Vietnam, and Indonesia, in line with international best practices, ASEAN data protection principles, and Google Play Store policies.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal data: Name, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Farming data: This includes information such as land size, crop type, farm location, yield records, input usage, farming practices, and observations (e.g., pest outbreaks, planting schedules, or weather-related notes). We may also collect other farming-related data that helps us deliver better agronomic insights and advisory services — such as water usage, soil condition, fertilizer applications, and harvesting activities.
  • Device or app usage data: Diagnostic logs, crash reports, and interaction data (if applicable), which help us improve the performance and user experience of the app.

3. Purpose of Collection

We collect and process your data for the following purposes:

  • Delivering personalized farming advice and agronomic recommendations
  • Supporting sustainability certification and environmental tracking
  • Improving app features and user experience
  • Generating insights and reports for partners (aggregated and anonymized)

4. Consent and Legal Basis

Your data is collected based on your informed and explicit consent. You must provide consent before any data is collected. You may withdraw your consent at any time through the app or by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your data only with your consent and for specific use cases, such as:

  • With government or NGO partners involved in agricultural development
  • With research, advisory, or certification agencies
  • With agribusiness stakeholders (e.g., input suppliers, sustainability partners, or buyers) for purposes that align with farmer support and ecosystem development

All shared data will be aggregated and anonymized where possible. We do not share personally identifiable information unless explicitly authorized by you.

6. Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on cloud infrastructure with robust security measures, including:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access control
  • Regular audits and monitoring

7. Cross-Border Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed on secure servers located in other countries. When transferring data across borders, we take steps to ensure it is handled in a way that respects local regulations and protects your privacy.

These steps may include data minimization, encryption, contractual safeguards, and ensuring that appropriate protections are in place. Where required, we obtain user consent or take additional compliance measures in line with local laws.

8. Use of Data for AI, Innovation, and Service Improvement

As a technology company focused on data and AI, AgriG8 leverages the information you provide to enhance our platform and services.

We may use data — including personal and farming-related inputs — to support activities such as:

  • Improving our platform functionality and user experience
  • Developing predictive tools, models, and digital advisory services
  • Generating reports, trends, and benchmarking information

While your data helps us build smarter solutions, please note:

  • We do not sell your personal or user-submitted data to third parties.
  • We may offer data-driven insights or aggregated outputs to partners, clients, or stakeholders in a way that does not identify individuals.
  • Any use of data for external purposes is done responsibly and in accordance with industry standards.

This approach is consistent with practices used by global digital platforms, where data is applied to build better experiences, inform strategy, and power innovation — always with respect for individual privacy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for specific uses
  • Contact us with any privacy concern

10. Access and Deletion Requests

To request access to your data or ask for it to be deleted, please contact us at:
📧 contact@agrig8.com
We will respond within 30 days.

11. Compliance with Google Play Policies

In accordance with Google Play's User Data policy:

  • We collect personal data only to support core features such as advisory and sustainability services
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely
  • You can opt out or request deletion at any time
  • This policy is displayed in-app and publicly accessible via our website

12. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via the app or website.

13. Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding this policy, contact:
📧 contact@agrig8.com

Kebijakan Privasi AgriG8

Tanggal Efektif: 21 Juli 2025

1. Pendahuluan

AgriG8 berkomitmen untuk melindungi data pribadi dan data pertanian Anda. Kebijakan Privasi ini menjelaskan cara kami mengumpulkan, menggunakan, menyimpan, dan membagikan data di Kamboja, Vietnam, dan Indonesia, sejalan dengan praktik terbaik internasional, prinsip-prinsip perlindungan data ASEAN, dan kebijakan Google Play Store.

2. Data yang Kami Kumpulkan

Kami dapat mengumpulkan jenis data berikut:

  • Data pribadi: Nama, nomor telepon, dan detail kontak lainnya.
  • Data pertanian: Ini mencakup informasi seperti ukuran lahan, jenis tanaman, lokasi pertanian, catatan hasil panen, penggunaan input, praktik pertanian, dan pengamatan (misalnya, wabah hama, jadwal tanam, atau catatan terkait cuaca). Kami juga dapat mengumpulkan data terkait pertanian lainnya yang membantu kami memberikan wawasan agronomi dan layanan konsultasi yang lebih baik — seperti penggunaan air, kondisi tanah, aplikasi pupuk, dan kegiatan panen.
  • Data penggunaan perangkat atau aplikasi: Log diagnostik, laporan kerusakan (crash reports), dan data interaksi (jika berlaku), yang membantu kami meningkatkan kinerja dan pengalaman pengguna aplikasi.

3. Tujuan Pengumpulan

Kami mengumpulkan dan memproses data Anda untuk tujuan berikut:

  • Memberikan saran pertanian yang dipersonalisasi dan rekomendasi agronomi
  • Mendukung sertifikasi keberlanjutan dan pelacakan lingkungan
  • Meningkatkan fitur aplikasi dan pengalaman pengguna
  • Menghasilkan wawasan dan laporan untuk mitra (diagregasi dan dianonimkan)

4. Persetujuan dan Dasar Hukum

Data Anda dikumpulkan berdasarkan persetujuan Anda yang terinformasi dan eksplisit. Anda harus memberikan persetujuan sebelum data apa pun dikumpulkan. Anda dapat menarik persetujuan Anda kapan saja melalui aplikasi atau dengan menghubungi kami.

5. Pembagian Data

Kami dapat membagikan data Anda hanya dengan persetujuan Anda dan untuk kasus penggunaan tertentu, seperti:

  • Dengan mitra pemerintah atau LSM (NGO) yang terlibat dalam pembangunan pertanian
  • Dengan lembaga penelitian, konsultasi, atau sertifikasi
  • Dengan para pemangku kepentingan agribisnis (misalnya, pemasok input, mitra keberlanjutan, atau pembeli) untuk tujuan yang sejalan dengan dukungan petani dan pengembangan ekosistem

Semua data yang dibagikan akan diagregasi dan dianonimkan jika memungkinkan. Kami tidak membagikan informasi yang dapat diidentifikasi secara pribadi kecuali diizinkan secara eksplisit oleh Anda.

6. Penyimpanan dan Keamanan

Data Anda disimpan dengan aman di infrastruktur cloud dengan langkah-langkah keamanan yang kuat, termasuk:

  • Enkripsi saat disimpan (at rest) dan saat transit
  • Kontrol akses
  • Audit dan pemantauan rutin

7. Transfer Lintas Batas

Data Anda dapat disimpan atau diproses di server aman yang berlokasi di negara lain. Saat mentransfer data lintas batas, kami mengambil langkah-langkah untuk memastikan data tersebut ditangani dengan cara yang menghormati peraturan lokal dan melindungi privasi Anda.

Langkah-langkah ini dapat mencakup minimalisasi data, enkripsi, perlindungan kontraktual, dan memastikan adanya perlindungan yang memadai. Jika diwajibkan, kami akan meminta persetujuan pengguna atau mengambil langkah-langkah kepatuhan tambahan yang sejalan dengan hukum setempat.

8. Penggunaan Data untuk AI, Inovasi, dan Peningkatan Layanan

Sebagai perusahaan teknologi yang berfokus pada data dan AI, AgriG8 memanfaatkan informasi yang Anda berikan untuk meningkatkan platform dan layanan kami.

Kami dapat menggunakan data — termasuk input pribadi dan yang terkait dengan pertanian — untuk mendukung kegiatan seperti:

  • Meningkatkan fungsionalitas platform dan pengalaman pengguna kami
  • Mengembangkan alat prediksi, model, dan layanan konsultasi digital
  • Menghasilkan laporan, tren, dan informasi tolok ukur (benchmarking)

Meskipun data Anda membantu kami membangun solusi yang lebih cerdas, harap perhatikan:

  • Kami tidak menjual data pribadi atau data yang dikirimkan pengguna kepada pihak ketiga.
  • Kami dapat menawarkan wawasan berbasis data atau hasil agregat kepada mitra, klien, atau pemangku kepentingan dengan cara yang tidak mengidentifikasi individu.
  • Setiap penggunaan data untuk tujuan eksternal dilakukan secara bertanggung jawab dan sesuai dengan standar industri.

Pendekatan ini konsisten dengan praktik yang digunakan oleh platform digital global, di mana data diterapkan untuk membangun pengalaman yang lebih baik, menginformasikan strategi, dan mendorong inovasi — selalu dengan menghormati privasi individu.

9. Hak Anda

Anda memiliki hak untuk:

  • Mengakses data pribadi Anda
  • Meminta koreksi atau penghapusan
  • Menarik persetujuan untuk penggunaan tertentu
  • Menghubungi kami jika ada masalah privasi

10. Permintaan Akses dan Penghapusan

Untuk meminta akses ke data Anda atau memintanya untuk dihapus, silakan hubungi kami di:
📧 contact@agrig8.com

Sesuai dengan kebijakan Data Pengguna Google Play:

  • Kami mengumpulkan data pribadi hanya untuk mendukung fitur inti seperti layanan konsultasi dan keberlanjutan
  • Kami tidak menjual data pribadi Anda
  • Data Anda dienkripsi dan disimpan dengan aman

  • Dữ liệu thiết bị hoặc dữ liệu sử dụng ứng dụng: Bao gồm nhật ký chẩn đoán, báo cáo lỗi và dữ liệu tương tác (nếu có), giúp chúng tôi cải thiện hiệu suất và trải nghiệm người dùng.

  • Cải tiến tính năng và nâng cao trải nghiệm người dùng trên ứng dụng
  • Tạo báo cáo và phân tích tổng hợp (ẩn danh) cho đối tác

Dữ liệu của người dùng được thu thập dựa trên sự đồng ý rõ ràng và minh bạch. Người dùng phải cung cấp sự đồng ý trước khi bất kỳ dữ liệu nào được thu thập. Người dùng có quyền rút lại sự đồng ý bất kỳ lúc nào thông qua ứng dụng hoặc bằng cách liên hệ với chúng tôi.

5. Chia sẻ dữ liệu

Chúng tôi chỉ chia sẻ dữ liệu của người dùng khi có sự đồng ý rõ ràng và trong các trường hợp cụ thể sau:

  • Với cơ quan chính phủ hoặc tổ chức phi chính phủ có liên quan đến phát triển nông nghiệp
  • Với tổ chức nghiên cứu, tư vấn hoặc đơn vị chứng nhận
  • Với các bên liên quan trong hệ sinh thái nông nghiệp (ví dụ: nhà cung cấp đầu vào, đối tác bền vững, người thu mua) nhằm hỗ trợ nông dân và phát triển hệ sinh thái

Tất cả dữ liệu chia sẻ sẽ được tổng hợp và ẩn danh hóa trong phạm vi có thể. Chúng tôi không chia sẻ thông tin định danh cá nhân nếu không có sự cho phép cụ thể từ người dùng.

Dữ liệu của người dùng được lưu trữ an toàn trên cơ sở hạ tầng đám mây với các biện pháp bảo mật mạnh mẽ, bao gồm:

  • Mã hóa dữ liệu trong quá trình lưu trữ và truyền tải
  • Kiểm soát truy cập nghiêm ngặt
  • Kiểm tra và giám sát định kỳ hệ thống

7. Chuyển dữ liệu xuyên biên giới

Dữ liệu của người dùng có thể được lưu trữ hoặc xử lý trên máy chủ đặt tại các quốc gia khác. Khi thực hiện việc chuyển dữ liệu xuyên biên giới, chúng tôi áp dụng các biện pháp nhằm bảo đảm dữ liệu được xử lý phù hợp với quy định địa phương và đảm bảo quyền riêng tư của người dùng.

Các biện pháp có thể bao gồm: giảm thiểu dữ liệu, mã hóa, điều khoản hợp đồng, và tuân thủ luật pháp hiện hành. Trong trường hợp cần thiết, chúng tôi sẽ xin thêm sự đồng ý từ người dùng hoặc thực hiện các biện pháp tuân thủ bổ sung phù hợp với luật pháp địa phương.
